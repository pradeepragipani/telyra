Reimbursement & Refund Policy

1. General Policy

Because digital art products are delivered electronically and cannot be physically returned, all purchases are generally non-refundable and non-reimbursable once the file has been downloaded or delivered.

However, reimbursements may be issued under specific circumstances outlined below.

2. Eligible Cases for Reimbursement

A customer may request reimbursement in the following cases:

a) Duplicate Payment
If a customer is charged twice for the same artwork due to a technical error.

b) Failed Delivery

If:

  • The digital file was not delivered, and
  • The issue cannot be resolved within 3 business days.

c) Corrupted or Defective File

If the downloaded file:

  • is corrupted,
  • Cannot be opened, and
  • A working replacement cannot be provided.

d) Unauthorized Transaction
If a payment was made fraudulently and verified by the payment provider.

3. Non-Eligible Cases

Reimbursement will NOT be granted in the following situations:

  • Change of mind after purchase
  • Lack of knowledge about file format or compatibility
  • Failure to read product description
  • Minor color differences due to screen settings
  • Buyer remorse
  • Purchased artwork used commercially without proper license

4. Request Procedure

To request reimbursement, customers must:

  • Submit a request within 7 days of purchase.
  • Provide:
    1. Order number
    2. Transaction ID
    3. Description of issue
    4. Supporting screenshots (if applicable)

Requests should be sent to: telyrasystemspvtltd2025@gmail.com

5. Processing Time

  • Requests are reviewed within 3–5 business days.
  • Approved reimbursements will be processed within 7–10 business days.
  • Refund timing may vary depending on the payment provider.

6. Partial Reimbursements

In certain cases (e.g., minor technical issues), the platform may offer:
